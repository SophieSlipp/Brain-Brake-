Eating

The recommended caloric intake each day is 2000, although this often varies with age, amount of activity and whether you want to lose gain or maintain weight. Each day you should also be eating 5 servings of vegetables, 4 serving of fruits, 6 servings of grains, and 3 servings of dairy.

Maintaining a healthy weight and diet can help reduce a person's risk for many health problems. With a healthy diet, the risk for type 2 diabetes and heart desease is greatly reduced, along with allowing a person to maintain a healthy blood pressure and cholesterol level. Eating food high in calcium can also keep your bones and teeth strong and slow. Eating properly can improve the immune system and prevent diseases leading to a healthier life overall.
