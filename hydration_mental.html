Some of the causes of dehydration can include diarrhea, vomiting, diabetes, burns, frequent urination, and sweating.

Dehydration can lead to less energy and a more irritable or cranky mood. Studies have shown that dehydration impaired metabolic activity in the brain, and that people exerted more effort to maintain a normal performance level as a result. Dehydration was found to result in significant changes in mood, cognitive function, and motor skills.
